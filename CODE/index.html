Introduction

Hello my name is Carlos Flores and I currently attend Richland College. I work as a floor and carpet installer in the Dallas area. I am taking this course to complete my program and look forward to complete my major later in the future. This will be my second coursce involving web design and I have discovered that I really enjoy it.

Interests

Movies like The Fast and The Furious franchise, the various Pokemon movies, and countless superhero action films were on constant rotation in my household. These movies and many like them continue to consume my time due to the exposure I received as a child.

Hobbies

A hobby passed down from my parents is gardening. I enjoy going to Home Depot and picking plants to later then plant. What fascinates me the most are fruits like tomatoes and jalapenos because they're easier to maintain and low cost.

Another hobby of mine is working on electronic devices. I enjoy disassembling devices because I like seeing the different components that make a device function. Plus the added challenge of remembering how to reassemble them.

Goals

My immediate goal is to graduate and receive my diploma. Getting past the hurdle of figuring out what I would like to do as a career was difficult. However, I now understand what it is I would like to do.

My secondary goal is to go to every state in the United States. I currently have most of the southeastern side of the country covered as well as Oklahoma and Nevada. Hopefully in the future can explore the rest of the country.
